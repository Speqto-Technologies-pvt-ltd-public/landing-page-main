What is IFYOne

IFYOne is a deflationary token where the percentages of the deflation and Autofarm functions can be changed by the Owner, this allows us to modify the percentages with the opinion of the community and what is more important to advance in the different phases of the project (you can see them on the road map).

This is not a token like the others since there are some objectives that will give a lot of value to the token, the project and the community and we will work hard to achieve them. In addition, all the liquidity of the team will be blocked so you should not worry about rugs pull since only 15% of the total supply is destined for the equipment wallet.

Autofarm

With the IFYOne functions, you will be able to receive rewards only for holding the token in your wallet, from each transaction you will receive 3% of the tokens.

These percentages can be changed by the owner, who will allow the community to choose the percentages.

Burn

The burn function is implemented to deflate the token and that the more transactions there are, the more value the token obtains.

The burn percentages can also be changed but will not be changed until the governance phase.

Liquidity Locked

With the pre-sale in Unicrypt, liquidity will be blocked for 6 months, so only 15% of total supply will be in the team's wallet.

After these 6 months, liquidity will be burned or blocked again to give maximum security to its investors.

IfyOne Governance

As indicated in our RoadMap, IFYOne, will become the governance token of the project, leaving behind the functions of Autofarm and Deflation, therefore the holders of the token will benefit a lot in other ways.

The holders of this token will receive different earnings from all the DApps, from the Farm and in many other ways that will be announced. These rewards can be claimed in a staking.
